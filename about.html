About Celeste Academy

Celeste Academy of Excellence is a registered educational institution dedicated to providing quality education from Grade RR to Grade 12. We are committed to nurturing young minds in a secure and innovative learning environment.

EMIS Registered: 99550231
Our Mission Our Values

Our Mission

To provide innovative and engaging education that empowers students from Grade RR to Grade 12 to become confident learners, critical thinkers, and responsible individuals through modern teaching methods and character development.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that sets the standard for academic excellence, innovation, and holistic student development in Polokwane, inspiring a lifelong love for learning and positive contribution to society.
